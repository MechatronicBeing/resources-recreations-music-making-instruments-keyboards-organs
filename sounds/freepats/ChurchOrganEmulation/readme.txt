Church organ emulation
----------------------

Version 2019-09-24

Sound samples and sound bank made by Roberto <roberto@zenvoid.org> for the
FreePats project. Recorded from Aeolus pipe organ emulator 0.9.0 on Debian
GNU/Linux operating system. Aeolus is a free software synthesizer made by Fons
Adriaensen: http://kokkinizita.linuxaudio.org/linuxaudio/aeolus/

This sound bank, in several formats and qualities, is available on the
FreePats project web pages: http://freepats.zenvoid.org/Organ/pipe-organ.html
